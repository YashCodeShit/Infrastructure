About Us

Rural Infrastructure Reporting is dedicated to transforming the infrastructure landscape of rural India. We provide a platform for citizens to report and address infrastructure issues, from roads and streetlights to sanitation and public services.

Our mission is to empower communities by amplifying their voices and fostering collaboration with local authorities. By leveraging technology and community engagement, we aim to create a sustainable, responsive system that ensures every concern is addressed swiftly and effectively.
